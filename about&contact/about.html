Our Mission

We, the students of Chitkara University, created Lifeline 24*7 to provide 24/7 ambulance services to blood donors, ensuring they reach nearby hospitals safely and on time. Our mission is to make blood donation a seamless and hassle-free experience, promoting a culture of generosity and social responsibility.

With Lifeline 24*7, you can rest assured that you'll receive prompt and reliable transportation to the nearest hospital, allowing you to focus on the selfless act of blood donation.

Our Activities

Since its inception, BloodConnect has developed a 360-degree solution to cater to the problem of blood shortage in the country. Our model revolves around these focal points:
  • Ensuring continuous, sufficient blood supply to blood banks through blood donation camps and weekly donations
  • Providing 24x7 emergency ambulance services to save lives and ensure quick hospital access for patients in need
  • Operating a 24x7 emergency helpline for those in critical need of blood and medical services
  • Increasing awareness through Street Plays, Info-Talks, Competitions, and Social Media Campaigns
  • Establishing a network of youth across the country to lead the blood donation movement
  • Collaborating with hospitals and blood banks to streamline the blood donation process and transportation
  • Organizing free health check-ups and wellness camps to educate people about the importance of blood donation
  • Offering donor benefits like post-donation health care services and informative sessions on safe blood donation
